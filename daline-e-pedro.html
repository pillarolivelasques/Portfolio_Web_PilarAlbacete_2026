Daline & Pedro

A soft, floral watercolor suite for a romantic beach wedding in Brazil.

This “Save the Date” was designed for a destination wedding in Florianópolis. The couple wanted something that felt intimate and artistic — so we painted the venue’s landscape and added a delicate monogram surrounded by tiny roses. The soft tones and handmade textures capture the lightness of their love and the natural beauty of the celebration.
