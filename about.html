Nice to meet you!

I'm Pilar — a Brazilian designer based in Madrid, passionate about watercolor, textures, and telling love stories through design.

My journey started when my brother was getting married. I decided to paint and create the entire visual identity of his wedding — just for fun, because I loved to paint. That ended up being my first real experience with watercolor, and it completely changed my life.

Since then, I've been designing custom stationery and illustrations with watercolor, especially for creative and expressive couples who want something different and meaningful.

I also love animals (seriously, I would adopt every single dog if I could 🐶), going for runs, playing sports, and I’m super competitive — challenges keep me motivated!

Thank you for being here. I hope my work speaks to your heart and helps tell your story in the most special way 💌
