Juliana & Alexandre

Silver Wedding Anniversary – Toledo, Spain

This project was created to celebrate a silver wedding anniversary in the charming town of Toledo, Spain. Inspired by the warm Spanish sun and the beauty of simplicity, we brought in watercolor oranges as the main visual element — a symbol of joy, abundance, and southern charm. The soft palette and handmade details reflect the couple’s lasting love and their journey together. Every piece, from the invitation to the signage, was designed to feel both elegant and fresh, capturing the essence of a timeless celebration.
