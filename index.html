Art that tells your story

Every couple carries a story worth painting — a sunrise ceremony, a childhood beach, a family chapel. My work begins there, with watercolor as the language and your memories as the muse.

Each commission starts as a conversation — about where you met, what you love, the small details only the two of you would notice. From there, I paint: soft washes of color that become invitations, save the dates, and keepsakes meant to be kept for a lifetime.

Whether it's a sunrise at Christ the Redeemer, a garden in Toledo, or a beach in Bahia, I bring the setting into the paper itself — so your guests hold a little piece of the place before they've even arrived.
