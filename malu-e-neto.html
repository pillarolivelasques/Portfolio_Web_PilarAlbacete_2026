Malu & Neto

Wedding stationary – Rio de janeiro, Brasil

This sunrise elopement at Christ the Redeemer was both sacred and emotional. Inspired by faith, love, and the soft colors of the early morning, we created a delicate watercolor suite filled with meaning. From scripture to scenic illustrations, every element was chosen to reflect the couple’s devotion and the intimacy of their moment.
